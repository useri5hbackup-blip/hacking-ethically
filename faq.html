Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions answered in one place.

What is Hacking Ethically?

Hacking Ethically is a learning platform designed to teach professional cybersecurity, penetration testing, and digital defense skills in a safe, legal, and structured way.

Do I need experience to start?

No. We offer full beginner‑to‑advanced learning paths, starting with basic networking and security fundamentals.

Is everything taught legal?

Yes. All lessons follow lawful cybersecurity practices. You learn ethical hacking, not illegal hacking.

Will I get course certificates?

Yes. After completing courses and quizzes, you will receive digital certificates verifying your training.

Do I receive hands‑on labs?

Yes. You will get safe virtual labs and simulations to practice real‑world skills without causing harm.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely. All courses are self-paced, and your progress is saved automatically.

Is this platform updated often?

Yes. New modules, labs, quizzes, and security topics are added regularly to keep up with industry changes.

Can I use mobile or tablet?

Yes. The entire platform is responsive and optimized for all devices.

How do I contact support?

Visit the Contact page and send us a message. We usually reply within 24 hours.
